The NEIL ER/LR Tracking Module allows Employee and Labor Relations Specialists within the Office of Human Capital to track cases for the following case types:

  • Conduct Issue
  • Grievance
  • Information Request
  • Investigation
  • Labor Negotiation
  • Medical Documentation
  • Performance Issue
  • Probationary Period Action
  • Third Party Hearing
  • Unfair Labor Practice
  • Within Grade Increase Denial/Reconsideration
